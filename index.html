/ introduction

Serena Khan is a multidisciplinary artist and creative director working between visual identity, digital installation, and narrative design. Based between New York and London, her practice focuses on quiet storytelling and structural clarity.

Selected clients & collaborators include cultural institutions, independent publishers, and design studios globally. She balances studio work with research-driven projects.

/ approach
  • Human-first design with emotional precision
  • Cross-disciplinary: print, digital, spatial
  • Emphasis on typography & materiality
  • Collaborative and iterative process
/ selected work
  • Fragments of Silence — short film & exhibition design (SXSW 2025)
  • Chroma Bodies — interactive installation, Milan Design Week award
  • Narrative Atlas — brand identity & book design for writers collective
  • Edge Conditions — public art commission, Brooklyn Bridge Park
  • Soft Monuments — editorial design & 3D visual essay
